# Product

<!-- impeccable:product-schema 1 -->

## Platform

web

## Users

Teens and young adults aged 13 and over who want low-pressure, character-led interactive fiction. They arrive to discover an appealing original companion, read a vivid opening scene, and continue the moment through conversation.

## Product Purpose

ReverieTale turns an original character and a short interactive scene into an ongoing conversation. Readers can browse companions, start a scene, keep talking after the scene ends, collect character moments, and create their own companions and stories.

## Positioning

The product begins with a compact, authored story moment and carries its details into a persistent conversation with that same companion. The companion remembers the scene and the reader's interaction over time.

## Operating Context

Discovery happens mainly on a phone or browser during spare moments. The primary path is discover a companion, enter their scene, then continue in chat. Returning readers resume active conversations, unlock companion photos, and explore new story worlds.

## Capabilities and Constraints

- Published companions have a portrait, bio, gender, tags, stories, likes, messages, and optional photo moments.
- Readers can browse by gender and tags, start or continue scenes, chat, listen to replies, like companions, and reveal selected companion photos with credits.
- The platform is 13+ and must retain age-appropriate content, clear safety boundaries, and moderation controls.
- The existing dark ReverieTale identity, character imagery, mobile navigation, shared footer, and responsive functionality remain product constraints.

## Brand Commitments

ReverieTale is warm, intriguing, contemporary, and character-first. It should feel like entering a story world rather than operating a generic social feed. The product uses real character portraits and scene imagery as the primary engagement device.

## Evidence on Hand

- A live character catalogue with portrait imagery, stories, tags, engagement counts, chats, and photo moments.
- Existing routes for discovery, story reading, chat, character profiles, companion galleries, and creation.
- No external customer testimonials, benchmark claims, or pricing claims should be invented for the home page.

## Product Principles

1. Make the next character encounter feel immediate and personal.
2. Let imagery create curiosity, then make the reader's next action obvious.
3. Keep story, conversation, and companion identity visibly connected.
4. Reward returning readers with momentum rather than repeating onboarding.
5. Keep discovery dense and mobile-native without making it visually noisy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

The site should keep text readable over imagery, provide clear keyboard focus, support responsive layouts, and avoid relying on color alone for meaning.
